iDaggorath Game -- Walkthrough
Commands

View
M: Show Map
V: Show 3D View

Arrows
Up: Move Forward
Down: Move Back
Left: Turn Left
Right: Turn Right

Levels
D: Climb Down
U: Climb Up
W: To 4th Level from 3rd
Q: To 3rd Level from 4th

Torches
P: Pine
L: Lunar
S: Solar
